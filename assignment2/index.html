Chicken

A family-owned restaurant, Peking instantly established a superior name for itself when it opened in 1978. Owner Miller Szeto continues the rich Asian tradition begun 30 years ago by his Chinese immigrant parents. His wife Bernadette and their two sons Matthew and Ryan also devote their time to help manage the business, marking a third generation of service to the community. The Szeto family warmly invites you to dine at Peking and experience Chinese cuisine at its finest.
